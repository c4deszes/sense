== Requirements

=== Power input

The power circuit shall accept DC input voltage from <<Hardware parameters,param_power_input_min>> to <<Hardware parameters,param_power_input_max>>.

The power circuit shall tolerate reverse or negative voltage
(with respect to the product's ground) conditions, with leakage less than
<<Hardware parameters,param_power_reverse_leakage>>.
