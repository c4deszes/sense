== Requirements

=== Operational voltage

The product shall be operational when the supply voltage is above
<<Parameters,param_power_low_error>> and below
<<Parameters,param_power_high_error>>

=== Reverse voltage

The product shall be operational when the supply voltage is restored after
a reverse or negative supply voltage event.

=== Voltage monitoring

The product shall monitor it's input voltage.

=== Current consumption

The product shall not consume more than <<Parameters,param_power_max_current>>
under any operating conditions.

The produt shall not consume more than
<<Parameters,param_power_max_sleep_current>> in sleep mode.
